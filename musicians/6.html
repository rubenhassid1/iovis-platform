About

We're a six-piece klezmer ensemble — violin, clarinet, accordion, bass, drums, and vocals — dedicated to bringing the joy and soul of Eastern European Jewish music to celebrations across Israel.

From the haunting melodies of the hora to the exuberant energy of the freylekhs, our music gets everyone — from grandparents to grandchildren — dancing together.

We also perform Israeli classics, Mizrachi hits, and modern celebration music. We're the complete musical package for Jewish celebrations.
